Magic Canvas

Welcome to Hogwarts!
You can build your own magic canvas with the tools on the left side. Choose a canvas size, a color and a symbol and one form of animation.
Now hit the generate button and your symbol will apear on the canvas. You can do this as many times as you like.
You can also clear your canvas with the delete button. Enter a name for your finished picture and press "save" to save it.
You can find your saved pictures on the right side.
