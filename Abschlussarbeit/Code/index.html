Magic Canvas

Welcome to Hogwarts! With the tools on the left side you can build your magic canvas.
First you can choose the size of your canvas. Then choose the color, a symbol and one form of animation.
Hit the generate button and your symbol will appear on the screen. You can do this as often as you want to.
You can clear your canvas with the delete button or enter a name for your picture and click the save button,
so you can continue your work later. You can find your saved pictures on the right side.
